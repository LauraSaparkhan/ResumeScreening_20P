Job Title: Senior Data Analyst / Data Science Lead

Location: Almaty, Kazakhstan (Hybrid or Remote Possible)

Job Type: Full-time

About the Role:

We are seeking a highly experienced and innovative Senior Data Analyst to join our team and lead data-driven initiatives that support research, education, and strategic decision-making. The ideal candidate will bring over 10 years of experience in data analysis, software engineering, and leadership—ideally within academic or research institutions. A background in computer science, strong analytical skills, and a passion for using data to drive innovation are essential.

Key Responsibilities:

Lead and mentor a team of data analysts and developers.

Design and implement statistical models to predict income and expenditure patterns in research and innovation projects.

Provide strategic insights and reporting to university boards, government agencies (e.g., National Science Foundation), and internal stakeholders.

Build, automate, and maintain dashboards and analytics platforms (e.g., Tableau) for various departments, including IT and contract management.

Develop and document best practices for analytics, application development, and security.

Ensure data quality through robust validation systems and audits.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate data systems and support software applications in education and research environments.

Qualifications:

Master’s degree in Computer Science (Professional Science Master’s preferred); Bachelor’s also in CS.

10+ years of experience in IT, with proven progression into data analytics and leadership roles.

Strong proficiency in SQL, SAS, JMP, SPSS, and Tableau.

Familiarity with C/C++, HTML, and JavaScript.

Experience in developing applications and dashboards for academic institutions or enterprise platforms.

Excellent communication and documentation skills.

Background in both backend development and statistical modeling.
